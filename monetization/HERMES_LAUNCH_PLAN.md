## 2. MARKETING STRATEGY: The "Supreme" Persona
The marketing will shift from "Technical Tool" to "Growth Engine." We aren't selling a CLI; we are selling *Influence and Scale*.

### Core Narrative
"Stop guessing what works. Stop spending 4 hours on a thread that flops. Use Hermes to detect what is already viral and deploy it in your voice instantly. Growth isn't a game of luck; it's a game of signals."

### Channels & Tactics
- **The "Viral Proof" Loop**: Every time a user successfully copy-trades a signal and it goes viral, we prompt them to share the "Before/After" (Original Signal vs. Hermes Rewrite).
- **Scarcity Engine**: "Limited Edition" signals. Only 50 users can copy-trade a specific high-value signal before it's retired to prevent saturation.
- **High-Signal Outreach**: Target "Ghostwriters" and "Growth Hackers" on X/LinkedIn. Position Hermes as their secret weapon to scale client accounts.
